# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-27

This Privacy Policy describes how **Binimise Labs Private Limited** (“Binimise”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, shares, stores, and protects information when you use the **Ambulance Driver** mobile application (“App”) and related services.

This App is intended for authorized personnel (e.g., drivers/staff) of participating organizations/tenants (e.g., hospitals). By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account and Profile Information

- Name, phone number, email address, tenant/organization code, and user identifiers.
- Authentication data (e.g., login events, mPIN/OTP verification status). We do not store OTP contents.

### 1.2 Location Information (While In Use)

- Approximate and/or precise location when the App is in use to support navigation, trip operations, and attendance workflows.
- We do **not** collect location when the App is closed or not in use.

### 1.3 Attendance and Identity Verification (Face)

- Face images and/or face templates (biometric identifiers) used for attendance and identity verification.
- Associated metadata such as timestamp and verification result.

### 1.4 Trip and Operational Data

- Trip details (pickup/drop locations, timestamps, status updates), vehicle/ambulance details, and operational logs.

### 1.5 Health Information (Patient Vitals)

- Patient vitals entered in the App such as temperature, blood pressure, blood sugar, heart rate, SpO₂, and consciousness/condition fields, when applicable.

### 1.6 Device and App Data

- Device identifiers and technical data such as device model, OS version, app version, and push notification token (e.g., FCM token) for notifications and security.
- Crash logs and diagnostic information to improve stability.

### 1.7 Files/Media (If You Use Related Features)

- Photos/images that you choose to upload or attach, if the App provides such features.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide core functionality (login, trips, attendance, navigation, notifications).
- Support customer/tenant operations (e.g., trip status, audit logs, reporting).
- Maintain security and prevent fraud/abuse.
- Improve performance, stability, and user experience (e.g., crash diagnostics).
- Comply with legal obligations and contractual requirements with tenants.

## 3. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information:

- With your organization/tenant administrators and authorized staff as required for operations.
- With service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., hosting, push notifications, maps services). These providers are permitted to process data only to provide services to us.
- If required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
- To protect rights, safety, and integrity of users, tenants, and our services.

We do not sell personal information.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for:

- providing the App and services,
- operational needs of your tenant/organization (including audit and reporting),
- legal and compliance obligations.

Retention periods may vary by tenant/organization policies and applicable law. When data is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it.

## 5. Data Deletion and Your Choices

### 5.1 Requesting Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us at:

- Email: **<support@binimise.com>**

Include:

- your registered phone/email,
- your tenant/organization name,
- and the deletion request details.

We may need to verify your identity and/or coordinate with your tenant administrator. We aim to process deletion requests within **30 days**, unless a longer period is required for legal, security, or contractual reasons.

### 5.2 Location Controls

You can control location permissions in your device settings. Disabling location may affect App features such as maps, navigation, trip workflows, and attendance.

## 6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information, including transmission security where applicable. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for authorized staff users and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. International Transfers

Depending on your tenant setup and service providers, your data may be processed in different regions. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
